# KPI Definitions

## Total Platform Spend (7 Days)

Sum of compute and storage cost across all clients and environments in the last 7 days.

**Purpose:** Executive visibility into short-term financial exposure.

---

## Active Cost Risk

Estimated spend associated with:

* Long-running jobs
* Failed workloads
* Inefficient compute usage

**Purpose:** Prioritize optimization actions.

---

## Untagged Asset Exposure

Cost generated by resources missing:

* Client ownership
* Component classification
* Environment tagging

**Purpose:** Enforce governance and accountability.

---

## Long-Running Workload Count

Number of jobs exceeding a defined duration threshold (e.g., >20 minutes).

**Purpose:** Detect inefficiencies and performance issues.

---

## Storage Risk Indicator

Datasets exceeding safe storage thresholds (e.g., >35 TB).

**Purpose:** Prevent uncontrolled storage growth.
